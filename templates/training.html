System Training

Train the OCR system to recognize specific prescription images with 100% accuracy

How Training Works

Training allows the system to learn from specific image-text pairs and produce exact outputs when those images are uploaded again.

  • Upload a prescription image
  • Enter the correct text that should be recognized from this image
  • The system will remember this image and always return the text you provided

Note: Once you train an image, the system will always recognize that exact image with 100% accuracy.
